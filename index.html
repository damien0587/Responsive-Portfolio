About Me


As a military brat and veteran, I’ve been all over the world. I have witnessed the impact that the tech industry has in almost every aspect of our modern lives. I also come from a huge family and I have little brothers looking up to me, which is why I’m taking the dive into such an important industry.


I became interested in web design when two of my roommates suggested that I give it a shot. I’m a recent psychology grad from Cal State East Bay, and the opportunity to expand my breadth of knowledge by jumping from a humanity to tech was very exciting. I’ve heard that tech is the future, but tech is NOW!
